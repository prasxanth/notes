Portfolio+ Powder
Dr. Greger · LDL-C / ApoB / LDL-P backbone · sprinkle lane
30 days
Batch
6 Tbsp
Daily
15–25m
Prep
Easy
Level
"Always-on" LDL-C reduction via viscous fiber + phytosterols + flax lignans. Keep it boring and consistent — this is the backbone.
Base Ingredients (180 Tbsp batch)
Ground brown flaxseed meal90 Tbsp
Psyllium husk powder (finely ground)30 Tbsp
Black sesame seed powder (raw)10 Tbsp
Sumac5 Tbsp
Micro-Mix (blend separately)
Taurine powder4 Tbsp
Plant sterols (powder)4 Tbsp
Artichoke extract powder5 tsp
Bergamot extract powder15 g
Kelp powder2 tsp
Filler (to reach 180 Tbsp total — choose one)
Wheat germ — if gluten-tolerant
Oat bran — gluten-free option
Defatted flax — neutral option
Preparation Steps
01
Build the base: Combine flax meal, psyllium, black sesame, sumac in a large bowl. Whisk 1–2 min until uniform. Optional: sift once to break psyllium clumps.
02
Make the micro-mix: In a separate small bowl, whisk taurine, plant sterols, artichoke extract, bergamot extract, and kelp until completely uniform.
03
Combine evenly: While stirring the base continuously, sprinkle micro-mix slowly across the surface. Keep stirring 60–90 seconds.
04
Finish: Add filler until you hit 180 Tbsp total. Transfer to airtight opaque jar. Label with name + date.
Daily Dose
2 Tbsp with breakfast
2 Tbsp with lunch
2 Tbsp with dinner
Mix into dal, sambar, soup, plant curd, chutney. Never dry-swallow.
Storage
Keep a 7–10 day working jar in the fridge
Bulk remainder sealed in the freezer (prevents flax rancidity)
Always use a dry spoon (psyllium clumps with moisture)
Troubleshoot
Too thick / gels fast
High psyllium + low liquid
Mix into soups or curd; add extra water; eat within 5–10 min of mixing.
Gas / bloating
Fiber jump + microbiome adjustment
Ramp: start at 1 Tbsp/meal for 7 days, then step up.
Off / rancid smell
Flax oxidation
Discard and rebuild. Store bulk in freezer; working jar in fridge.

BALT Powder
Dr. Greger · Black Cumin · Amla · Long Pepper · Turmeric · swallow lane
Daily
Freq
1 tsp
Dose
3–5m
Prep
Easy
Level
Strongest-tasting, highest-leverage powders delivered without tasting them. Swallow lane only — keep BALT separate from meal-sprinkle powders.
Daily BALT Spec (1 tsp total)
Black cumin (Nigella) powder¼ tsp
Amla powder⅓ tsp
Long pepper (Pippali) powder⅛ tsp
Turmeric powder¼ tsp
Ratio B:A:L:T = 3:4:1.5:3
Delivery Method — Edible Film Wonton (Preferred)
01
Ensure dry hands — film is instant glue with moisture.
02
Place powder mound in center of edible film sheet.
03
Fold tightly like a wonton — tight corners reduce leaks.
04
Lightly dip edge in water to seal fully. Don't half-seal and hope.
05
Swallow with plenty of water — don't sip, commit.
Alternate Delivery
Option A: Fill capsules (prefilled or DIY)
Option B: Curry slurry — mix powders with a little water, chug immediately
Capsules > film when mobile (flights, hotels, shared spaces)
Ayurvedic Energetics
Nigella — pungent + bitter, warming; ↓Kapha, ↓Vata (small dose), may ↑Pitta if overdone
Amla — sour + astringent, cooling; ↓Pitta, ↓Vata; can be drying if taken very dry
Pippali — pungent, warming; ↓Kapha, supports Agni; may ↑Pitta if pushed
Turmeric — bitter + pungent, warming; ↓Kapha, clears Ama; may ↑Vata if very dry
Net: warming, Agni-supporting, Kapha-reducing. Amla = cooling counterweight.
Variations
Gentler start: Pippali ⅟₁₆ tsp
Stronger push: Pippali ⅙ tsp (upper practical limit)
Amla-forward (cooling bias): Amla ½ tsp + Pippali ⅟₁₆ tsp
Troubleshoot
Film tears
Damp hands or heavy payload
Dry hands; use larger sheet; reduce payload; double-layer if needed.
Burning / heat sensation
Pippali too stimulating initially
Cut pippali to ⅟₁₆ tsp for 5–7 days, then restore.
You dread taking it
Method friction
Switch to capsules — compliance beats elegance.

SEP Smoothie Cubes v1.5
β-glucan · Resistant starch · Protein · Bile acid binding
28
Cubes
4 cubes
Per smoothie
35m+
Active prep
12–16h
Soak
Delivers exactly 2 Tbsp Portfolio+ and high protein via whole-foods. The oat bran stacking is intentional — concentrated β-glucan for aggressive triglyceride repair.
Overnight Soak
Steel-cut oats7 Tbsp
Chia or basil seeds7 Tbsp
Oat bran3 Tbsp
Fenugreek powder1–1½ tsp
Lemon juice or ACV1 tsp
Protein Base
Silken tofu (soft)2½–3½ cups
Unsweetened soy milk2–3 cups
Water to total1.5 L
SEP Actives
Portfolio+ powder14 Tbsp
Flaxseed powder7 Tbsp
Amla powder7 tsp
Turmeric powder2 tsp
Ginger powder2 tsp
Long pepper (Pippali)⅛ tsp
Vanilla extract1½ tsp
Cinnamon (optional)1 tsp
Preparation Steps
01
Overnight soak: Combine oats, chia/basil seeds, oat bran, fenugreek, lemon/ACV. Cover with water. Soak 12–16 hours.
02
Light cook (critical): Drain. Transfer to saucepan + 2–2.5 cups fresh water. Simmer 5–6 min. Do not overcook — goal is soft and lightly viscous, not gummy.
03
Curcumin dispersion: While still warm, stir in turmeric, ginger, amla, long pepper. Let sit 5 min. Cool 5–10 min more to increase retrograded resistant starch.
04
Primary blend: Add warm mixture + silken tofu to blender. Add soy milk + water to reach 1.5 L. Blend 20–30 sec longer than usual to prevent oat bran clumps.
05
Add remaining actives: Add Portfolio+, flax, vanilla, cinnamon. Blend until homogeneous. Portfolio+ is added last to protect its sterols.
06
Freeze: Pour immediately into silicone trays → 28 large cubes. Tofu must be blended before freezing to maintain cream texture.
Daily Smoothie Assembly
4 cubes from freezer
Frozen berries to ⅓ blender volume
Water or soy milk to blend
Optional: cinnamon + erythritol
Never Add
❌ Dates, banana, or mango — high insulin noise
❌ Nut butters or coconut cream — fat stacking
❌ Firm tofu — becomes crumbly and breaks emulsion (silken only)

Nitric Oxide Core Powder
Vascular · Endothelial · Mitochondrial efficiency
30
Doses
½ tsp
Daily
10m
Prep
Easy
Level
"You can't amplify a signal you can't deliver." NO signaling enhances downstream delivery of sulforaphane, polyphenols, and exercise signals.
Monthly Batch Ingredients (30 doses)
Beetroot powder7½ tsp (2½ Tbsp)
Arugula (rocket) powder7½ tsp (2½ Tbsp)
Daily dose½ tsp total
Preparation
01
Measure beetroot and arugula powders into a bowl. Mix thoroughly for uniform nitrate distribution.
02
Transfer to an airtight jar. Done — this is your 30-day batch.
03
Daily: Measure ½ tsp. Add to smoothie after blending or to cool/room-temperature food. Consume shortly after mixing.
Critical Rules
Once daily. Consistency builds the effect.
Avoid hot foods or hot drinks around dosing — heat degrades nitrates.
Avoid antiseptic mouthwash ±2 hours — oral bacteria are required for nitrate → nitrite conversion. This is non-negotiable.
Best timing: 30–60 min before light or moderate exercise.
Oral Microbiome Protection
Prefer non-antibacterial toothpaste
Tongue scraping is fine; antimicrobial rinses are not
Dental cleanings can blunt effect for 24–48 hours
What "Working" Feels Like
Subtle warmth, mild "pump," or easier breathing during activity
Occasionally a light head rush when standing quickly (vasodilation)
Often no acute sensation — this is normal. Benefits accumulate over weeks.
Troubleshoot
No noticeable effect
Inconsistent dosing or oral pathway disrupted
Ensure daily use; avoid antiseptic mouthwash ±2 hours.
Headache or flushing
Vasodilation sensitivity
Reduce dose to ¼ tsp for a few days; hydrate well.
GI discomfort
Dose too concentrated or empty stomach
Take with food or dilute further.

Batch
1–2g

Level
Essence without sludge: aromatics via water (Path A), lipophilic actives via milk micro-decoction (Path B). Moderate caffeine + calm-alert botanicals.
Base Blend Ingredients (200g batch)
Shatavari root60g (12 Tbsp)
Dry ginger48g (9½ Tbsp)
Fennel30g (6 Tbsp)
Ceylon Cinnamon20g (4 Tbsp)
Turmeric16g (3¼ Tbsp)
Cardamom12g (2½ Tbsp)
Licorice6g (1¼ Tbsp)
Marshmallow Root6g (1½ Tbsp)
Nutmeg2g (½ tsp)
Path A — Spice-Infusion Sachet (bright & clean)
01
Place 1–2 g base in a fine paper tea bag.
02
60–90 ml water @ 92–95°C. Steep 3–4:30 min. Dunk 3–5× at start → rest → finish by lifting & pressing gently. Do not wring.
03
Brew coffee liquor separately. Pour 60–90 ml infusion into cup, add 120–170 ml coffee. Stir 5–10 s.
04
Optional body: 10–20 ml milk or 1–2 g inulin. No sugar.
Optional Add-Ins (choose 1–2)
Gotu Kola 0.5–1.0 g — calm attention; minimal sedation; water-extractable
Tulsi 0.5–1.0 g — anxiolytic without dulling; pairs with coffee
Rose petal 0.2–0.3 g — softens acidity; pleasant top note
Path B — Micro-Ksheerapaka Concentrate
—
Batch (120–150 ml, keeps 3–4 days): 6 g base blend + optional Ashwagandha 2–3 g + 120 ml milk + 120 ml water + sunflower lecithin 0.4–0.5 g (after straining).
—
Gentle simmer 12 min → strain fine → cool → whisk lecithin → refrigerate.
—
Per cup: 15–20 ml (AM) or 20–30 ml (late AM / early PM). Warm gently; do not re-boil.
Coffee Dose (AeroPress / filter)
Calm Focus: coffee 12–14 g, water 160–170 g @ 88–90°C, 1:45–2:00 total
Standard: coffee 15 g, water 225 g @ 88–90°C, 2:00
Seasonal Tweaks
May–Jun: Skip Path B most days; fennel-forward Path A; rose water 2–3 ml in cup
Nov–Jan: Use Path B 20–30 ml; optionally +2 g ginger to the monthly 200 g batch
Sep: Ashwagandha only in Path B; ≤20 ml AM to avoid "flatness"
Mar–Apr: Pepper in Path B only; Path A steep ≤4:30 for clarity
Troubleshoot
Muddy / gritty
Coarse bag or bag left in too long
Fine paper bag; steep in water only; remove bag on time.
Jitters
Caffeine peak too high
Coffee 12–14 g + bypass architecture; add 15–20 ml Path B.
Flat / sedated
Too much licorice or AM ashwagandha
Reduce licorice; keep AM Path B ≤20 ml; shift PM.

More pepper/ginger for cold; more fennel/licorice for heat. Make one jar close to the Winter profile — then adjust dose and steep time by season.

Base Prep (Both Seasons)
01
Lightly toast whole spices on low for 30–60 s until fragrant. No browning.
02
Cool fully. Grind coarse. Add nutmeg, ginger powder, Arjun, licorice after toasting (during grinding or directly to pot).

Masala jar: grind coarse, store airtight up to 4 weeks. Label: "Winter: 1 tsp/cup, daily: ¾ tsp/cup".
❄ Winter Brew
01
2 heaped tsp winter masala (≈1 tsp/cup) + 2 cups water to saucepan.
02
Simmer gently 5–7 min.
03
Add milk and tea (if using); simmer 2–3 min more. Strain; sweeten as desired.
Heat dial: max = 1.5 tsp pepper + 1 tsp pippali per 2 cups. Most people do better at ≈1 tsp total pepper family.
☀ Summer Brew
01
1 level tsp summer masala (≈½ tsp/cup) + 2 cups water.
02
Gentle simmer only 3–4 min (avoid rolling boil).
03
Add milk and tea; simmer 1–2 min. Strain; lightly sweeten.
Cool dial: still too hot → drop pepper to a pinch, bump fennel to 1.5 tsp.
Safety Limits
Pepper family max: 1.5 tsp pepper + 1 tsp pippali / 2 cups (occasional only)
Nutmeg: ≤¼ tsp per pot — sedating
Licorice: avoid if high BP, edema, or on diuretics
Cardamom: if 10–12 pods feels loud, drop to 6–8
Dosha-Oriented Tweaks
Vata: Gentle heat, ginger + a bit of pepper, little pippali, keep fennel + licorice
Pitta: Summer/neutral; fennel + cardamom + star anise lead; keep pepper, pippali, cloves, nutmeg very low or none
Kapha: Winter or slightly hotter; more ginger, moderate pepper, minimal licorice
Troubleshoot
Too hot / harsh
Pepper high
Drop pepper to pinch; add fennel 1–1.5 tsp; shorten simmer.
Flat / dull
Over-boiled milk or spice
Gentle simmer; add aroma late; shorten total time.
Curdled alt-milk
Boil too hard
Simmer gently; add plant milk off heat; brief warm only.

Polyphenol Pulse v1.1
AMPK · Autophagy · Hormetic Signaling · 2× per week only
≈8
Doses
2×/wk
Freq
5m
Prep
Easy
Level
"Signals should arrive, resolve, and be missed." Used daily, it becomes noise. Used sparingly, it drives adaptation.
Batch Ingredients (≈8 doses)
Natural cacao powder (non-alkalized)4 tsp
Grape skin powder (or finely ground raisins)3 tsp
Ceylon cinnamon powder1 tsp
Per dose½–1 tsp
Preparation
01
Measure cacao, grape skin (or raisin powder), and cinnamon into a mixing bowl.
02
Mix thoroughly to ensure even polyphenol distribution.
03
Transfer to an airtight container. Store dark and cool. Use within 4–6 weeks.
Pulse Rules — Non-Negotiable
2× per week maximum. Space doses at least 48 hours apart.
Do not use daily — daily exposure blunts the hormetic response.
Prefer lighter-calorie or lower-protein days.
Skip on heavy training, feasting, or illness days.
Avoid pairing with large protein or high-fat meals.
If you crave it daily, reduce to 1×/week for 2–3 weeks — that's the hormesis talking.
Consumption Options
Cold smoothie / shake — add after blending; consume immediately
Warm (not hot) liquid — stir into warm plant milk or water; avoid boiling
Yogurt / plant curd — mix into cool base; eat directly
Water slurry (minimalist) — mix with water; drink quickly
Sourcing Notes
Cacao: Must say "natural" or "raw" — avoid Dutch-processed, alkalized, smooth cocoa. (Navitas Organics, Terrasoul, Viva Naturals)
Cinnamon: Ceylon only (lower coumarin load)
Grape skin powder: Micro Ingredients or BulkSupplements grape skin powder; no extracts
Avoid: cocoa drink mixes, sweetened cacao blends, "antioxidant blend" powders, capsules
Troubleshoot
Jittery or overstimulated
Dose too high or cacao sensitivity
Reduce to ½ tsp; avoid late-day use.
GI discomfort
Too concentrated or taken empty
Take with light food or dilute further.
Diminishing effect over time
Too frequent use
Reduce to 1×/week for 2–3 weeks, then resume 2×/week.
